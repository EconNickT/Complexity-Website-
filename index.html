About Complexity Insights

Applying cutting-edge science to economic and financial analysis.

Traditional economic models often fall short because real-world markets are not in equilibrium. They are complex, dynamic, and constantly evolving.

At Complexity Insights, we embrace this reality. We use advanced computational methods like agent-based modeling and network science to provide a clearer picture of market behavior. Our approach helps you anticipate change, manage risk, and find opportunities that others miss.

Based in the Washington, DC area, we partner with government agencies, financial firms, and research institutions to tackle their most complex challenges.

Our Services

Specialized expertise in complexity economics and financial systems analysis

Agent-Based Modeling

We build simulations of your market to test how different strategies or policies might play out in the real world.

Network Analysis

We map your industry's financial and economic networks to identify key players, risks, and opportunities for growth.

Market Microstructure

We analyze trading patterns and liquidity to understand how prices are really formed in today's fast-paced markets.

Financial Risk Assessment

Our models stress-test portfolios and institutions against unexpected market shocks, revealing hidden vulnerabilities.

Systemic Risk Analysis

We identify large-scale risks in financial markets that can arise from feedback loops and interconnectedness.

Policy Impact Analysis

We simulate the effects of regulatory changes and monetary policy to predict their true impact and potential side effects.
