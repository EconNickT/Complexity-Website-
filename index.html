About Complexity Insights

Applying rigorous complexity science to economic and financial systems analysis

Traditional economic models often assume equilibrium conditions and rational agents making optimal decisions. Yet real-world markets exhibit path dependencies, feedback loops, and emergent behaviors that these frameworks cannot capture.

At Complexity Insights, we employ advanced computational methods and complexity theory to analyze economic and financial systems. Our approach integrates agent-based modeling, network science, and empirical data analysis to provide actionable insights for policymakers, financial institutions, and corporations.

Based in the Washington, DC area, we work with clients across government agencies, financial services firms, and research institutions to understand market dynamics, assess systemic risks, and evaluate policy interventions in complex adaptive systems.
